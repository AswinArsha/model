Paneer

Paneer is a popular Indian cheese made by curdling milk with lemon juice or vinegar. It is a versatile ingredient used in many Indian dishes, including curries, appetizers, and desserts. Paneer has a mild and creamy taste, and its firm texture allows it to be easily sliced or cubed. It is a good source of protein and calcium.
